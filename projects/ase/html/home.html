BIOS consortium RNA-seq Allele-Specific Expression (ASE) browser

We have studied Allele Specific Expression (ASE) in 3,818 blood samples from BIOS, a large Dutch biobank consortium, to assess the effects of rare, pathogenic and high impact variants on allelic expression imbalance. We have identified 53,964 genetic variants showing significant (Binomial FDR ≤ 0.05) ASE. The observed variants can be queried using this website.
